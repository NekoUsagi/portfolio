About Us

Speciality Coffee for Every Taste

For more than 40 years Java Coffee & Tea Co. of Houston, Texas; has been supplying quality coffees and teas to businesses and individuals nationwide. All of our coffee is fresh and roasted daily. ​ Java Coffee & Tea Co. offers next business day delivery to most Texas cities including, Houston, Sugar Land, The Woodlands, Kingwood, the greater Dallas, Austin and San Antonio areas. In Houston, be sure to visit our retail shop at 2727 Fondren Road & Westheimer.

This Is What We Do

In 1979 Java Coffee was born with the idea of providing a high quality coffee to a reduced group of customers. In order to accomplish that, we centered our effort in 5 guiding principles: - Stay close to our customers; they are the reason why we are here. - Build a reliable network of green coffee providers with the same high standard as ours. - Knowledge of the natural characteristics of each of the regions where we get our green coffee - Find the optimum roasting temperature to enhance the natural notes of the coffee according to its origin. - Roast small batches. That's why it's always fresh.
